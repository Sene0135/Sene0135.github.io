Wasantha's Family Childcare GALLERY SERVICES

Wasantha's Family Childcare is a well-established family child care business that has been proudly serving families in Northridge, California since 1999. With over two decades of experience in the industry, the team at Wasantha's is highly skilled in providing exceptional care and education to children of all ages. They offer a warm, loving, and safe environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. The business is led by Wasantha, who has a passion for early childhood education and is committed to providing the highest level of care possible. Wasantha's Family Childcare is dedicated to creating a positive and nurturing environment that promotes the physical, social, emotional, and cognitive development of every child in their care.

Rose and Charlotte were two young children who were taken in by their aunt and uncle since birth. Their aunt and uncle provided them with a loving home, but they struggled to care for them while working two full time jobs.Ms. Wasantha's childcare gave them a safe and supportive environment for Rose and Charlotte to learn, play, make friends, and develop literacy skills. They thrived with the help of caring teachers and their aunt and uncle.

Danny and Isaac were two young boys who were taken into Wasantha's care when they were just four years old. Prior to this, they had been homeschooled, so it took them a few weeks to adjust to their new environment. However, with the caring and nurturing guidance of Wasantha and her team, the boys were able to settle in and become comfortable in their new surroundings. As they began to adapt to their new routine, Danny and Isaac quickly developed strong friendships with the other children in Ms. Wasantha's care. They were grateful for the love and support they received, and felt like they were part of a big family.
